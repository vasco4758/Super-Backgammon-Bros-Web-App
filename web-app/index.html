How to Play

Each player moves their 15 checkers (Marios or Goombas) around the board in opposite directions.

The board is divided into four quadrants, with each player having a home board and an outer board.

Roll two dice to see how many points (pips) you can move your checkers. The left dice plays first, to swap the order click on the dice.

You can move one checker per die or split the roll across multiple checkers. Move the checkers by clicking on the checker stack.

A point with two or more of your opponent's checkers is blocked and cannot be landed on.

A lone opponent checker can be hit and sent to the bar; it must re-enter the board before you make other moves.

Your goal is to move all your checkers into your home board and then bear them off, bringing the flag to the top of the pole.

The first player to bear off all their checkers, or to eat the opponent's checkers 5 times and make them lose all their hearts, wins the game!

Be careful to avoid the pipe, that triangle cannot be used during the entire game.

Keyboard Controls

Press R to roll the dice, S to swap dice, I to toggle instructions, use the left and right arrow keys to navigate between checker stacks, and press Enter or Space to move a checker from the selected stack.
